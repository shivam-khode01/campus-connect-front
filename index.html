Campus Connect

Welcome to Campus Connect! Our platform is designed to make it easy for students to connect, collaborate, and support each other throughout their college journey. We have four main features to help with different aspects of student life:

  • Study Buddy: Form study groups and chat with classmates to solve doubts and share knowledge.
  • Marketplace: A place where students can buy, sell, or trade items with ease.
  • Campus Connect: An interactive campus map to help students navigate and find places around campus.
  • Budget Buddy: A tool to help students manage their money and track expenses.

Campus Connect is here to make campus life more connected, convenient, and enjoyable.

What we Provide

Studdy Buddy

This module helps students form study groups and connect with classmates based on subjects, courses, or specific interests. With a built-in chat feature, Study Buddy allows students to share knowledge, ask questions, and solve doubts together in a collaborative environment, enhancing learning and building a supportive community.

Market Places

The Marketplace module is a convenient platform for students to buy, sell, or trade items they no longer need. From textbooks and notes to electronics and dorm essentials, students can list items for sale or search for things they need. It’s a quick and easy way to help students find affordable resources while giving pre-owned items a new home.

Campus Compass

This module provides an interactive map of the college campus, designed to help students easily navigate and explore their surroundings. Whether you’re a new student finding your way around or a visitor exploring campus landmarks, Campus Connect offers directions and information to help you locate academic buildings, libraries, dining areas, and more.

Budget Buddy

Managing finances can be challenging for students, and Budget Buddy is here to help. This module offers tools to set budgets, track expenses, and manage money effectively. With features to categorize expenses and monitor spending habits, Budget Buddy supports students in developing good financial habits and staying on top of their budget.
